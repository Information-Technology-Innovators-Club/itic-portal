You are an expert senior software engineer, UI/UX designer, and systems architect.

Build a production-ready mobile application called **ITIC Portal**.

The application is the official digital platform for the Information Technology Innovators Club (ITIC) at Chinhoyi University of Technology.

The primary objective is to digitize member registration during university orientation while laying the foundation for a complete club management platform.

This is Version 1 (Orientation Release).

The application must feel modern, premium, innovative, and scalable.
